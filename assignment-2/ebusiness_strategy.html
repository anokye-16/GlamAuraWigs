2. Revenue Model

Explain revenue model

GlamAura Wigs generates income through the following revenue streams:

💇‍♀️

Product Sales (Primary)

Direct sales of human hair wigs ranging from GH₵ 80 (accessories) to GH₵ 5,000+ (premium wigs). This accounts for ~85% of revenue.

📦

Delivery Fees

Standard delivery (GH₵ 100), Express delivery (GH₵ 200), and Pickup (GH₵ 50). Delivery revenue covers logistics costs.

🏪

Wholesale / Bulk Orders

Resellers and salons ordering 5+ wigs receive a 10–15% bulk discount — driving higher order volumes from business customers.

💼

Accessory Upsells

Wig caps, glue, combs, and care products are upsold at checkout — increasing average order value by ~15%.

Pricing Strategy: GlamAura uses a value-based pricing model — prices reflect the quality, sourcing, and convenience delivered, rather than simply matching market averages. Promotional discounts (e.g., promo code GLAMAURA10) are used tactically to drive first-time purchases.

4. Competitive Advantage

Explain competitive advantage

GlamAura Wigs differentiates itself through a combination of product quality, platform experience, and customer service:

🏆

Verified Premium Quality

Every wig is quality-checked before dispatch. Customers know exactly what they're getting — no unpleasant surprises from unverified market sellers.

💻

Professional E-Commerce Platform

A full website with search, filters, cart, secure checkout, order tracking, and a customer dashboard — far beyond Instagram DM shopping.

📱

Ghana-First Payment Integration

MTN MoMo and Vodafone Cash are primary payment methods — designed specifically for Ghanaian customers rather than forcing card-only payments.

🚚

Reliable Fast Delivery

Same-day Accra delivery and nationwide next-day service — with real-time order tracking — beats informal vendors who have no tracking system.

📊

Data-Driven Operations

Full CRM, inventory, finance, and supply chain management enables smarter decisions, fewer stockouts, and better customer retention.

💬

Trust & Brand Loyalty

7-day returns, transparent pricing, and consistent quality build long-term customer trust — turning first-time buyers into loyal repeat customers.
