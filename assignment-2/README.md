# Assignment 2: Business Model and E-Business Strategy

## Guiding Question 2
What business model and digital strategy will your e-business adopt?

## Value Proposition
GlamAura delivers premium quality human hair wigs at accessible prices through a fast, convenient online platform — eliminating the need for physical market visits. Key promises: verified quality, instant styling confidence, fast Ghana-wide delivery, secure MoMo payment, 7-day returns, 24/7 availability.

## Revenue Model
- **Product Sales (85%)** — Wigs priced GH₵ 80 (accessories) to GH₵ 5,000+ (premium)
- **Delivery Fees** — Standard GH₵ 100, Express GH₵ 200, Pickup GH₵ 50
- **Wholesale / Bulk Orders** — 10–15% discount for 5+ unit orders
- **Accessory Upsells** — Wig caps, glue, combs upsold at checkout (+15% average order value)

## Competitors
| Competitor | Type | GlamAura Advantage |
|-----------|------|-------------------|
| Market Wig Vendors | Physical/Informal | Online ordering, delivery, quality guarantee |
| Instagram Wig Sellers | Social Media | Professional checkout, secure payment |
| Jumia Ghana | Online Marketplace | Curated quality, specialist brand |
| Tonaton Listings | Classifieds | Full e-commerce, cart, order tracking |

## Competitive Advantage
- Verified premium quality with QC before dispatch
- Professional e-commerce platform (not WhatsApp DMs)
- Ghana-first payments (MTN MoMo primary)
- Reliable fast delivery with order tracking
- Full CRM and supply chain data for smarter decisions
- 7-day return policy builds trust and loyalty
