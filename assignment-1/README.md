## Business Idea: GlamAura Wigs

**GlamAura Wigs** is a B2C online wig retail store based in Accra, Ghana. The platform allows customers across Ghana to browse, select, and purchase premium human hair wigs online — with MTN MoMo, Vodafone Cash, card, and bank transfer payment options, and nationwide delivery.

### Business Concept
An e-commerce website selling 24+ styles of premium human hair wigs, synthetic wigs, and wig accessories — targeting women aged 18–45 across Ghana.

### Products/Services Offered
- Premium human hair wigs (Body Wave, Straight, Curly, Deep Wave, Kinky Curly, Bob styles)
- Coloured fashion wigs (Blonde, Burgundy, Ombre, Chocolate Brown, Highlight, Platinum)
- Wig accessories (Wig caps, lace glue, styling combs)
- Delivery service (Standard, Express, Pickup)
- Customer support via WhatsApp, email, and contact form

### Target Customers
- Working women (25–45) wanting low-maintenance professional styles
- University students (18–27) following hair trends
- Brides and event guests
- Women experiencing hair loss
- Wig resellers and market sellers
- Ghanaian diaspora shopping online

### Why Online is Suitable
- High mobile penetration (80%+) in Ghana enables mobile commerce
- Wigs are visual products — high-quality images drive purchase decisions online
- Removes geographic limits — nationwide reach without physical stores
- 24/7 availability matches busy customer schedules
- Lower overhead than physical retail — better prices for customers
- Integration with MoMo makes checkout accessible to all Ghanaians
