Assignment 1: Identification of E-Business Idea

Guiding Question: What is your proposed e-business idea?

1. Business Concept

Describe the business concept

GlamAura Wigs is an online premium wig retail e-business based in Accra, Ghana. The business sells high-quality human hair wigs, synthetic wigs, and wig accessories directly to customers through a fully functional e-commerce website.

The platform allows customers to browse a curated catalog of 24+ wig styles — ranging from Brazilian Body Wave and Silky Straight wigs to Kinky Curly and Blonde Lace wigs — select their preferred hair length and texture, add items to a cart, and complete a secure checkout process using mobile money, card payment, or bank transfer.

GlamAura also features a customer dashboard for order tracking and profile management, as well as a comprehensive admin/back-end dashboard that handles CRM, inventory, finance, ordering, and supply chain operations.

🌐

Platform Type

B2C (Business-to-Consumer) E-commerce Website

📍

Base Location

Accra, Ghana — delivering nationwide

🛍️

Business Model

Online Retail (Direct-to-Consumer)

📱

Payment Methods

MTN MoMo, Vodafone Cash, Card, Bank Transfer

4. Why Online? Suitability for an E-Commerce Platform

Explain why the idea is suitable for an online platform

The wig and hair extension market is highly suitable for e-commerce for several compelling reasons:

📸

Visual Product Appeal

Wigs are highly visual products — high-quality product images and detailed descriptions drive purchasing decisions online just as effectively as in-store.

📱

Growing Mobile Commerce in Ghana

Ghana's mobile penetration exceeds 80%. Customers already shop via WhatsApp and Instagram — a dedicated website professionalises this behaviour.

🌍

Wide Geographic Reach

An online store removes geographic limits — GlamAura can serve customers in Kumasi, Tamale, Takoradi and internationally without a physical presence in each city.

💰

Lower Overhead Costs

Operating online eliminates the high rental costs of physical retail space, allowing GlamAura to offer competitive prices and invest in product quality.

🕐

24/7 Sales Availability

The website operates around the clock, allowing customers to browse and purchase at their convenience — even at 2AM before a morning event.

📊

Data-Driven Business Decisions

E-commerce platforms generate valuable data on customer behaviour, popular products, and sales trends — enabling smarter inventory and marketing decisions.

📦

Efficient Supply Chain

Digital ordering integrates directly with inventory management and supplier systems, reducing manual errors and improving fulfilment speed.

🔐

Secure & Trusted Payments

Integration with MTN MoMo and card processors gives customers safe, familiar payment options — increasing conversion and trust.

In summary, GlamAura Wigs leverages the digital shift in Ghanaian consumer behaviour to build a scalable, profitable, and customer-centred online wig retail business that serves thousands of women across the country.
